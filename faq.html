Registration

When is registration for NU World Cup 2017 and/or NU Women's World Cup 2017?

Registration is Monday, February 13th at 12 pm.

How many players can I register?

The minimum number of players is 10 and max is 16.

What information do I need to register?

If you are the team captain, you will need the names and emails of the 10 to 16 players that you plan on registering and 3 possible country names you would like your team to be called.

What happens if team registration fills up before I register?

Your team will be added to the waitlist. Get to registration quick, it fills up fast!

If I don't have a team can I still register for the tournament?

No worries! There is individual player registration for both the Co-Ed and Women's tournaments! That means that teams that are in need of more players will be calling on you to join them. You can find the registration form for individual registration in the same place as for teams. Check out the register tab.
Game Play

How many players can be on the field at once?

The games are run 7 on 7. If you have less than 5 players than you forfeit the match.

How many club players can play?

In the Co-Ed tournament there is a max of 2 (PAST or PRESENT) club players allowed on a team roster. For the Women's tournament there is a max of 3 club players allowed.

Can a player play in the Co-Ed and Women's tournament?

Yes! That's allowed, but if both your teams are playing at the same time you will have to choose to play for only one.
General

How much does it cost to play?

For the Co-ed tournament each player pays $10 and for the Women's each player pays $5. Each team is asked to raise $100 for Gonzo Soccer as well. Check out Gonzo to learn more about the program we are donating to!

Where and when is the tournament?

Both tournaments will be on Friday April 21st and Saturday April 22nd, on the fields by the Lakeside.

What is the tournament structure?

The Co-Ed tournament will be 40 teams divided into 10 groups. There will be group stages and then elimination bracket, just like FIFA World Cup. The Women's tournament will have 8 teams split into 2 groups.
